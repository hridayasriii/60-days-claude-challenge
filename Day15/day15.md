# Day 15 – Astrology & Life Analysis

## Objective
Explored how AI can generate a structured astrology-based life analysis from user-provided birth details.

## Tasks Completed
- Opened Claude AI
- Entered personal birth details
- Generated the astrology report
- Reviewed career, wealth, relationship, and life pattern sections
- Saved screenshots of the generated report

## Key Learnings
- Learned how AI can organize astrological information into a structured report.
- Explored AI-generated career and life insights.
- Understood that such reports are interpretive rather than scientifically verified and should not be the sole basis for important life decisions.
